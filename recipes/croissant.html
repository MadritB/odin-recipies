Let's make some Croissant

Description

Today we're conquering our fears and making homemade croissants! If you're about to run away screaming, I understand. I'm not sugarcoating it: croissants aren't easy. Croissants require time, patience, and a lot of rolling. However, just because this recipe is advanced doesn't mean that YOU have to be an advanced baker to try it. You can absolutely handle this recipe.

Prep Time: 45 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 9

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Chocolate

Steps:

Here's a brief overview of what you can expect when you make this Croissant:

  1. Make easy dough from butter, flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and milk.
  2. Roll out dough into a large rectangle.
  3. Make butter layer and enclose inside dough.Roll out the dough again, fold it back together again.
  4. Roll out the dough one more time, fold it back together.
  5. Shape the croissants and finally bake it
